Live Music Sundays

Sunday Swing

Big Band, Jazz, Swing, Soul & More — Every Sunday at KS.

Looking for something to do in Ottawa on a Sunday evening?

KS on the Keys in South Keys is bringing the energy every Sunday this spring and summer with Sunday Swing — live music nights featuring some of Ottawa's best bands playing swing, jazz, Latin, big band, blues, fusion, funk, soul, and a little bit of rock and roll.

Just $5 at the door · No dress code · Come as you are

Upcoming Performances

May 24th, 2026 — Aardvark Big Band

Kick off Sunday Swing with the Aardvark Big Band — a powerhouse ensemble bringing the full big band experience. Expect horn-driven arrangements and infectious rhythms.

May 31st, 2026 — Apex Jazz Band

Smooth, soulful, and endlessly listenable — perfect for a relaxed Sunday evening with great food and good company.

June 7th, 2026 — Grey Jazz Big Band

Rich horns, tight rhythms, and all the charm of a golden era dance hall right here in South Keys Ottawa.

June 14th, 2026 — Standing Room Only

Expect a high-energy setlist spanning swing, funk, soul, and rock and roll — one of Ottawa's most exciting live acts.

June 21st, 2026 — Big Band Ottawa (Father's Day)

Bring Dad out for a night of live music, great food, and cold drinks — the perfect Father's Day celebration.

What to Expect at Sunday Swing

Every Sunday Swing evening runs from 5:00 PM to 7:30 PM at KS on the Keys. Here is what you can expect:

  • 🎵 Live Music

    Ottawa's best bands every Sunday.

  • 🍽 Full Food Menu

    Dine while you enjoy the show.

  • 🍸 Full Bar Service

    Cocktails, beer, wine, and soft drinks.

  • 💃 Dance Floor

    Open all night for swing, jazz, and Latin.
