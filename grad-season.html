Celebrate Your Grad at KS on the Keys

Private dining, unforgettable memories, and food worth celebrating — since 1971

Graduation is one of life's biggest milestones. At KS on the Keys, we have been helping Ottawa families celebrate the moments that matter most for over 54 years. Whether you are planning an intimate dinner for close family or a full celebration for friends and loved ones, our team is here to make your grad night truly unforgettable. From our exclusive Grad Special buffet to our private event spaces, everything you need is right here in South Keys Ottawa.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where is KS on the Keys located?

A: We are located at 1029 Daze Street, South Keys Ottawa — with free parking on site. Easy to find, easy to get to.

Q: What is the Grad Special buffet?

A: The KS Grad Special is an all-inclusive buffet at $28 per person for groups of 20 or more. It includes penne in cream sauce, assorted sandwich wraps, BBQ meatballs, spring rolls, Thai chicken bites, assorted pizza, cookies, brownies, assorted soda and citrus infused water.

Q: When is the Grad Special available?

A: The Grad Special buffet is available from mid-May through the end of June 2026 — right through Ottawa's busiest graduation season.

Q: Do you have private event spaces for grad parties?

A: Yes — we have three private event spaces accommodating groups from 20 to 110 guests. Visit our event spaces page for full details or call us at 613-521-0498.

Q: Is KS on the Keys halal?

A: Yes — KS on the Keys is a fully halal restaurant. Every item on our menu including the Grad Special buffet is halal certified.

Q: Can I walk in during grad season without a reservation?

A: Walk-ins are always welcome. However reservations are strongly recommended during grad season as we fill up quickly in June.

Q: How do I book a private grad event?

A: Contact us at RSVP@ksrestaurant.ca or call 613-521-0498 to discuss availability, event space options, and menu customization. You can also reserve online at tbdine.com.

Q: Is there parking at KS on the Keys?

A: Yes — free parking is available on site for all guests.

Q: Can I customize the menu beyond the Grad Special?

A: Absolutely. Our full event and catering menu is available for any group. Contact us to discuss options.

Q: What areas of Ottawa do you serve?

A: We are located in South Keys Ottawa near Bank Street and Hunt Club Road — convenient for guests coming from all across Ottawa and the surrounding area.
