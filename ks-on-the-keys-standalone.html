Our Story

Ottawa South's Favourite Since 1971

Founded in May 1971, KS on the Keys has been proudly serving our Ottawa community for over 50 years. What started as a neighbourhood gem has grown into one of South Ottawa's most beloved dining destinations.

Our tradition of excellence continues with a modern twist on diverse world cuisines — from classic Canadian comfort food and authentic pizzas to vibrant Middle Eastern plates and Asian-inspired dishes.
