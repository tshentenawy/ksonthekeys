Official Partnership

Shoot from the Keys

Brought to You by KS on the Keys Restaurant & Event Spaces

KS on the Keys is an Official Partner of the Ottawa Blackjacks

KS on the Keys — Ottawa's premier halal restaurant and event venue in South Keys — is proud to announce our official partnership with the Ottawa Blackjacks of the Canadian Elite Basketball League. At every Blackjacks home game this 2026 season at The Arena at TD Place, watch for our signature fan segment: Shoot from the Keys, brought to you by KS on the Keys Restaurant and Event Spaces.

How It Works

At the end of every Ottawa Blackjacks home game, selected fans get their chance to shine. Step onto the court, walk to the top of the key, take your shot — and you could walk away with a KS on the Keys gift card in your hand.

  • First 5 shots made — win a $50 KS on the Keys gift card, valid on food and drinks.
  • All other contestants — receive a 25% discount gift card, valid on food at KS on the Keys.

Every single contestant wins something. Every game. All season long.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Shoot from the Keys?

Shoot from the Keys is an official fan segment at every Ottawa Blackjacks home game. Selected fans are invited onto the court to take a shot for a chance to win a KS on the Keys gift card.

Q: Do I have to make the shot to win something?

No — every contestant wins. Make the shot and win the $50 gift card. Miss the shot and still walk away with a 25% discount card valid on food at KS on the Keys.

Q: Where is KS on the Keys located?

We are at 1029 Daze Street, South Keys Ottawa — just minutes from The Arena at TD Place on Bank Street. Free parking is available on site.
