Do you offer halal options? ▾
Yes! We offer halal pepperoni on our pizzas, as well as halal options across other dishes. Please ask your server when ordering and they'll be happy to guide you through the halal options on our menu.
Are there vegetarian and vegan options? ▾
Absolutely! We have a wide variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes across our menu — from vegan Thai Cauliflower Bites and Falafel Plates to Vegan Spring Rolls, Hummus & Pita, and vegan pizza options with vegan cheese. Look for the VEGAN badge on our menu items.
Do I need a reservation? ▾
Walk-ins are always welcome! However, for groups over 10 people, we strongly recommend making a reservation in advance. You can book online through our website or call us at (613) 521-0498.
Do you offer gluten-free options? ▾
We offer a gluten-free pizza crust option. Please note that KS on the Keys is NOT a gluten-free facility, so cross-contamination is possible. All toppings are gluten-free except sausage and ham. Please speak to your server if you have serious gluten allergies.
Can you accommodate large groups? ▾
Yes! We have private spaces that can accommodate groups from 10 up to 120+ guests. Our Grand Event Room holds up to 110 guests, our Mezzanine holds up to 60, and our Private Board Room holds up to 20 guests. For large group bookings, email RSVP@ksrestaurant.ca.
Do you offer catering off-site? ▾
Yes! We offer full catering services both at our venue and off-site throughout the Ottawa area. Our team handles everything from setup to cleanup. Contact us at RSVP@ksrestaurant.ca to discuss your needs and get a custom quote.
Are you open on Mondays? ▾
We are generally closed on Mondays for regular dining. However, for large group events and private bookings, we can open on Mondays by special arrangement. Please reach out to us at RSVP@ksrestaurant.ca to arrange a Monday booking.
Do you have a weekend buffet? ▾
Yes! We offer a Breakfast Buffet and a Brunch Buffet on weekends. Check our website for current buffet schedules and pricing, or call us for the latest information.
